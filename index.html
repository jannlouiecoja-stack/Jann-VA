Common Questions

What do you need from me to get started?
Just a clear breakdown of your current bottlenecks and access to the platforms you want me to manage. I am highly proactive—once I have the initial instructions and SOPs, I leverage internal knowledge bases and root-cause analysis to figure things out independently without interrupting your day.
How do you handle time tracking?
I operate on an outcome-based philosophy. Instead of heavy micromanagement or screen-monitoring software, I prefer using a standard Daily Time Record (DTR) and focusing strictly on deliverables. I work exceptionally fast; my goal is to complete your daily requirements accurately and efficiently, ensuring you receive a full day's worth of high-quality results without the friction of micromanaging the clock.
What are your working hours and availability? (PHT)
I currently work a night shift (Thursday to Monday, 11:00 PM - 8:00 AM PHT).

For Part-Time / Hourly Projects: I am fully available on Tuesdays and Wednesdays, plus daily core hours from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM for meetings and syncs.
For Full-Time Opportunities: I am actively looking for my next big career move and am ready to render a 30-day notice to my current employer for the ideal, long-term full-time partnership.
Do you handle inbound phone calls or voice support?
No, I specialize strictly in non-voice back-office operations, data entry, inbox management, and chat/ticket support. This allows me to maintain deep focus on complex administrative tasks. However, I am fully available for weekly voice or video syncs to align on our goals!
